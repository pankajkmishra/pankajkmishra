Welcome to my personal website! I’m an Applied & Computational Geophysicist with research interests in computational geophysics, magnetotelluric methods, multiphysics inversion, and uncertainty quantification. Currently, I work as a Senior Scientist at the Geological Survey of Finland (GTK). I’m a big believer in collaborative research, so feel free to reach out if you think we can create something impactful together. Thank you for visiting—I hope you find my work insightful and useful.
